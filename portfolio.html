Project Background

Company Profile

Ndawonga Trading and Projects Co-operative Ltd is a 100% Black-Owned Civil Engineering and Waste Management company based in Johannesburg, South Africa. The company holds Level 1 B-BBEE certification and is CIDB registered.

As a B2B and B2G company, Ndawonga specializes in large-scale construction projects, infrastructure development, and comprehensive waste management solutions for government and corporate clients.

Project Objectives

The primary goal was to develop a professional corporate website that serves as a powerful business tool for:

  • Generating qualified leads for large-scale projects
  • Establishing credibility with government entities and corporate clients
  • Showcasing certifications, team expertise, and project portfolio
  • Streamlining tender application processes
  • Attracting potential joint venture partners

Business Challenge

Before this project, Ndawonga lacked a professional online presence, which limited their ability to:

  • Effectively showcase their B-BBEE Level 1 certification to potential clients
  • Display their extensive project portfolio to tender committees
  • Provide easy access to required documentation for tender applications
  • Generate qualified leads through digital channels
  • Establish credibility in the competitive construction industry
  • Compete with established players who had professional websites

Project Overview

The Ndawonga website is a comprehensive digital solution designed to position the company as a leader in the South African construction industry while leveraging their B-BBEE certification as a competitive advantage.

Database Architecture

The website utilizes a robust MySQL database with the following key tables:

  • Users: Admin and editor accounts with role-based permissions
  • Projects: Complete project portfolio with categories and status tracking
  • Services: Company services with display ordering and active status
  • Team: Team member profiles with positions and contact information
  • Certifications: B-BBEE, CIDB, and other certifications with expiry tracking
  • Tenders: Current tender opportunities with closing dates
  • Messages: Contact form submissions with inquiry categorization

Security Implementation

Data Protection
  • Password hashing using bcrypt algorithm
  • SQL injection prevention with PDO prepared statements
  • XSS protection through input sanitization
  • CSRF token implementation for forms
  • Session management with secure configurations
Access Control
  • Role-based authentication (Admin/Editor)
  • Secure file upload validation
  • Input validation on all user-submitted data
  • Secure admin panel with login requirements
  • Error handling without exposing sensitive information
